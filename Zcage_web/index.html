Welcome to ZCAGE

We can solve the credit problem of small, medium and micro enterprises for you

The development of small, medium-sized and micro enterprises needs financial support, but project support such as loans needs risk assessment. On the one hand, enterprises need to provide evidence to banks and other institutions to prove their potential. On the other hand, banks need to verify the correctness of this evidence to assess risks. Using this zero knowledge proof system can solve this problem perfectly.

You can use this system to let large enterprises provide you with the scope of cooperation between the enterprise and these large enterprises, or check the parameters such as the power consumption range of the enterprise from China power grid, so as to judge the living environment and health status of the enterprise and conduct risk assessment.
