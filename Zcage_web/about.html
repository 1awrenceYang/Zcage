About us

We are committed to providing a zero knowledge proof system to solve some problems of financing difficulties and weak reliance of small, medium-sized and micro enterprises. On the one hand, you can use this system to prove the current survival and health of the company to others; On the other hand, the certifier can achieve the purpose of proof without divulging privacy. In this case, you can easily assess the health status of the enterprise.

There are many indicators to evaluate the health status of an enterprise. For example, you can ask the power company about the power consumption of the enterprise to judge whether it operates normally; You can ask other large enterprises about the cooperation with the enterprise to judge the capital situation of the enterprise. Since our circuit is open, the result can be supervised in many aspects, so the proof is convincing.
